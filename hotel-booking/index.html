WELCOME TO THE ORIENTAL JADE HOTEL


The Oriental Jade Hotel – a member of Oriental Hospitality Group (OHG) - offers 120 premium guest rooms and suites with stunning views of Hoan Kiem Lake, Hanoi Cathedral and Hanoi Old Quarter. Located on Hang Trong Street, the hotel provides guests with a convenient base from which they can explore Hanoi Old Quarter within walking distance.

At The Oriental Jade Hotel, customer satisfaction remains our top priority. We are committed to delivering bespoke services that go beyond guests’ expectations. Let us bring you a memorable stay in Hanoi!

WHAT TRAVELLERS SAID ABOUT US

"On New Year's Day, my family decided to travel to Hanoi and The Oriental Jade Hotel is where we booked. Its location is perfect, 1 minute away from cathedral, famous Pho in Hanoi and Hoan Kiem lake. The pool is on the roof and it was great. The service is nice and the staff are friendly and helpful. The high light is the cleanliness and the bed."

Laura Wright from England, stayed 2019

"Even though it was winter, I tanned on the roof by the pool a few days a week. It was great! It was sunny just about all day until 5pm. The view was amazing - right next to Hoan Kiem lake. It's also next door to the beautiful cathedral to take pictures at. The hotel is next to the main street that closes down on weekends for pedestrians to walk around. Since it's not directly on the busy street, it's quieter. Breakfast was also quite good. There was a decent selection and the service was wonderful."

James from USA, stayed 2018
